The Infinity Stones are fictional cosmic artifacts that appear in Marvel Comics and the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU). In the Marvel universe, these stones are immensely powerful and each represents a different aspect of the universe. When combined, they grant the holder god-like powers and control over various fundamental forces. In the Marvel Cinematic Universe, there are six Infinity Stones, each with its own unique color and power, which are the Space Stone (Blue/Tesseract), the Mind Stone (Yellow/Vision), the Reality Stone (Red/Aether), the Power Stone (Purple/Orb), the Time Stone (Green/Eye of Agamotto), and the Soul Stone (Orange/Vormir). The Infinity Stones play a central role in several films, including "The Avengers", "Guardians of the Galaxy", "Avengers: Infinity War", and "Avengers: Endgame". The primary antagonist, Thanos, seeks to collect all six Infinity Stones to carry out his plan of balancing the universe by wiping out half of all life.
